Hi, I'm Ibrohim and welcome to my personal blog! The internet is littered with these and most of their authors are far better informed on the topics they choose to write about. I made this website because a) I learned to make websites just in time for my previous project to fall through, so I had all this poorly written code lying around, wanted to clean it up, and b) GitHub hosts one of these for free (albeit with name restrictions), so why not! I like to tell myself that this blog is meant to help me learn through writing and that it effectively doubles as a portfolio showcase, but really it's a place for me to pacify my OCD by messing with different designs and typography on a near-daily basis. It's hard to tell because the website feels like you're staring directly into the Sun, but hey, minimalism! I'm kind of into niche things and I like to write (not to be mistaken for 'good at'), so if that's also your thing, have a look around!
